Welcome to the House of Legacy

Born in the foothills of the Sangre de Cristo Mountains, Casa Legado represents more than just a brand; it is a way of life. Founded in 2024 in Santa Fe, New Mexico, we fuse the rugged durability of traditional ranch wear with the refined palette of the Southwest.

From our signature canvas vests to our hand-forged branding irons, every piece is designed to withstand the elements while offering timeless style.
